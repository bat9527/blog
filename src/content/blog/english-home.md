## 🎭 第三部分：场景对话 Dialogues

### 场景一：早晨忙碌 Morning Rush

> **A:** Wake up, honey! The alarm went off ten minutes ago.  
> **A：** 醒醒亲爱的！闹钟十分钟前就响了。

> **B:** Ugh, just five more minutes, please.  
> **B：** 呃，再睡五分钟，求你了。

> **A:** No, you're going to be late for work. Breakfast is on the table.  
> **A：** 不行，你上班要迟到了。早餐在桌上了。

> **B:** Okay, okay. I'm getting up. Is there any coffee?  
> **B：** 好的好的，我起来了。有咖啡吗？

> **A:** Yes, I just brewed a fresh pot.  
> **A：** 有，我刚煮了一壶新鲜的。

---

### 场景二：做晚饭 Cooking Dinner

> **A:** What should we eat for dinner tonight?  
> **A：** 今晚我们吃什么？

> **B:** How about pasta? We have some tomato sauce and mushrooms.  
> **B：** 意大利面怎么样？我们有些番茄酱和蘑菇。

> **A:** Sounds good. Do we have any onions?  
> **A：** 听起来不错。我们有洋葱吗？

> **B:** Let me check the fridge... Yes, we have one left.  
> **B：** 我看看冰箱... 有的，还剩一个。

> **A:** Great. Can you chop the onions while I boil the water?  
> **A：** 太好了。我烧水，你能切一下洋葱吗？

> **B:** Sure, no problem.  
> **B：** 没问题。

---

### 场景三：周末大扫除 Cleaning Day

> **A:** Look at this mess! We need to clean the house today.  
> **A：** 看看这乱的！即使必须打扫屋子了。

> **B:** I know. I'll vacuum the living room and wipe the furniture.  
> **B：** 我知道。我去吸客厅的地，擦擦家具。

> **A:** Okay, I'll tackle the bathroom and do the laundry.  
> **A：** 好，那我去搞定卫生间和洗衣服。

> **B:** Can you also take out the trash? It's full.  
> **B：** 你能顺便把垃圾倒了吗？满了。

> **A:** Will do. Let's get started so we can relax later.  
> **A：** 没问题。我们开始吧，这样一会能休息下。

---

### 场景四：欢迎客人 Welcome Guests

> **A:** (Ding-dong) I'll get it!  
> **A：** （门铃响）我去开门！

> **B:** Hi! Come on in. Welcome to our new home.  
> **B：** 嗨！快请进。欢迎来我们的新家。

> **C (Guest):** Wow, it looks amazing! More spacious than I imagined.  
> **C（客人）：** 哇，看起来太棒了！比我想象的宽敞。

> **A:** Thanks. Make yourself at home. Can I get you something to drink?  
> **A：** 谢谢。别客气当自己家。喝点什么吗？

> **C:** Water would be great, thanks.  
> **C：** 水就好，谢谢。

## 📝 第四部分：实用句型 Useful Sentence Patterns

### 1. 询问物品位置 Asking Location

- Where is the...? ...在哪？
- Have you seen my...? 你看到我的...了吗？
- Where did you put the...? 你把...放哪了？

### 2. 请求做家务 Requests

- Can you help me with...? 你能帮我...吗？
- Could you please clean...? 你能打扫一下...吗？
- It's your turn to... 轮到你...了。

### 3. 表达感受 Feelings at Home

- It feels so cozy here. 这里感觉真温馨。
- I love relaxing on the sofa. 我喜欢在沙发上放松。
- This room serves as a study. 这个房间用作书房。
